About AI Fitness Trainer

What is AI Fitness Trainer?

AI Fitness Trainer is an intelligent fitness application that uses advanced pose estimation technology to provide real-time feedback on your exercise form and technique.

Key Features

  • Real-time Pose Detection: Uses MediaPipe to detect and track your body positions
  • Form Analysis: Validates your exercise form against proper technique guidelines
  • Rep Counting: Automatically counts repetitions and sets
  • Instant Feedback: Get real-time tips and corrections
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your workout history and improvements

Supported Exercises

  • Bicep Curls
  • Squats
  • Push-ups
  • Planks
  • Shoulder Press

Technology

Built with:

  • Python Backend (Flask)
  • MediaPipe for Pose Detection
  • OpenCV for Video Processing
  • Modern Web UI (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript)

Tips for Best Results

  • Use good lighting for optimal pose detection
  • Keep the entire body visible in the camera frame
  • Start with beginner exercises to learn the system
  • Follow the form guidance provided by the AI
